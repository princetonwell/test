Why Sitting Is the New Smoking: A Deep Dive into Sedentary Lifestyle Risks

In today's digital era, our lifestyles have become significantly more sedentary. Sitting for prolonged periods has been dubbed 'the new smoking' due to its extensive health risks. This article explores the dangers associated with a sedentary lifestyle and how Princeton Wellness Centre can help mitigate these risks through holistic health practices.

Long hours of sitting impact our health in several detrimental ways. From increased risks of heart diseases, diabetes, to mental health struggles, the impact is far-reaching...
