What is Registered Massage Therapy?

Registered Massage Therapy (RMT) is a regulated healthcare profession in Ontario. RMTs are trained in anatomy, soft-tissue assessment, therapeutic techniques, injury management, and clinical treatment planning.

Unlike general relaxation massage, RMT sessions are designed to treat:

  • Muscle tension & stiffness
  • Posture-related pain
  • Stress-related headaches
  • Sports & overuse injuries
  • Chronic tightness affecting daily movement

Each treatment includes assessment, targeted therapy, and personalized home-care suggestions.
