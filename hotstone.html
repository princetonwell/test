What is Hot Stone Massage?

Hot Stone Massage is a specialized massage technique that combines traditional manual therapy with the therapeutic benefits of heat. Smooth volcanic stones are heated to a comfortable temperature and used as an extension of the therapist's hands, allowing for deeper muscle release with less pressure.

This treatment helps:

  • Relax the entire body
  • Warm tight muscles
  • Improve circulation and energy flow
  • Reduce anxiety and mental fatigue
  • Ease chronic stiffness and tension

The result is a deeply restorative and luxurious wellness experience.
