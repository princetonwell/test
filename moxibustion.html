Comfortable, Relaxing Heat Therapy

Moxa can be applied in several gentle ways:

  • Indirect moxibustion with moxa sticks held above the skin
  • Moxa cones on a protective medium (such as ginger or salt)
  • Warming boxes placed over the abdomen or back
  • Moxa combined with acupuncture needles (warm needle technique)
