What is Ear Candling?

Ear candling involves placing a specially designed, hollow candle at the opening of the ear. When lit, the candle creates a warm, soothing environment that may help:

  • Relieve pressure
  • Encourage relaxation
  • Reduce discomfort associated with sinus congestion
  • Create a feeling of clarity and balance

While not a medical treatment or replacement for professional healthcare, ear candling is commonly used as a complementary wellness practice for relaxation and comfort.
